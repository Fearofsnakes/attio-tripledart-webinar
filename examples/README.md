## The comparison move (the payoff of the session)

After the skills run, put the real ads next to `outputs/`:

- Does the ad's **audience** match the ICP the skill defined? Or is it aimed wider/vaguer?
- Does the ad's **claim** ladder up to the messaging stack's POV and value prop? Or is it a feature line invented for the campaign?
- Is the ad fighting in the **white space** the positioning audit found, or in the crowded middle where everyone says "AI-native"?

That gap, real ad vs foundation, is the whole point. It shows the room what inherited inputs cost them.
