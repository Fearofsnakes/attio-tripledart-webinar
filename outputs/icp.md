# Attio — ICP definition

> Output of the `icp-definition` skill, run on the Attio context. Pre-built for the webinar demo. June 2026.

## Act 1 — The layered ICP

**Firmographic**
- VC-backed startups and high-growth tech, seed to Series C, ~5 to 200 employees.
- Modern, AI-forward stack (Slack, Linear, Notion). Often AI-native companies themselves.
- Founder-led or early GTM-led revenue motion.

**Behavioral**
- Outgrowing a spreadsheet, or actively frustrated with a rigid CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce).
- Hires a first RevOps / GTM ops person and wants tooling that bends to them.
- Builders: they customize their tools, use APIs, and resent software that boxes them in.

**Psychographic**
- Believe their go-to-market is unique and should not be forced into a template.
- Value control, speed, and a clean data model over a long feature list.
- Early adopters of AI who expect software to do work, not just store records.

**Language (their actual words)**
- "data model," "custom objects," "our workflow"
- "rip out HubSpot," "a CRM that doesn't get in the way," "set it up the way we sell"

## Act 2 — Anti-ICP (who to exclude from spend)

- Large enterprises that lead with procurement, SSO, and heavy compliance first.
- Non-technical SMBs who want a turnkey, opinionated CRM and will not configure anything.
- Teams already deep in and happy with Salesforce (switching cost too high, not in pain).

## Act 3 — Signal map (make it actionable for paid + outbound)

| Signal | Where to catch it | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Fresh funding round | Crunchbase, news | Target seed–B founders |
| First RevOps / GTM ops hire | LinkedIn jobs | High-intent, tooling decision incoming |
| "Switching off HubSpot/Salesforce" chatter | Reddit, X, communities | Pain-aware retargeting |
| Job posts naming Attio / Notion / Linear | Job boards | Stack-fit lookalikes |

**For a media buyer:** this is the difference between bidding on "CRM" broadly and bidding on *funded, builder-led startups in active tooling pain*.
