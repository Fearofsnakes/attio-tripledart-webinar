## POSITIONING FOUNDATION
> Attio is the CRM for builder-led startups who've outgrown the spreadsheet but won't be boxed in by a legacy CRM. Unlike Salesforce or HubSpot (which bend you to their model) or Notion and Airtable (flexible, but not a real CRM), Attio gives you a fully customizable data model on a real CRM backbone — so you set it up the way you sell, in an afternoon, without an admin, and it still scales at 200 people.

## THE 5 LAYERS

### 01 · POV — Disagreement 4/5
> CRMs were built to make every company sell the same way. But for a startup, **how you sell is the edge** — and a CRM that forces you into someone else's playbook quietly flattens the one thing that makes you win. The next decade of GTM goes to teams running on a system that bends to their motion, not the other way around.

*Usage: About page, thought leadership, brand campaigns, slide 1 of any deck.*
*Why it scores 4: Salesforce/HubSpot actively argue the opposite — their value prop is "adopt our proven, standardized pipeline." This POV calls that standardization a liability for startups.*

### 02 · VALUE PROPOSITION — CUT 5/4/5
> Attio is the CRM that bends to how you actually sell — flexible like a Notion doc, real like a CRM — so you set it up your way in an afternoon, without an admin.

*Usage: Homepage hero (H1), ad headlines, email subject lines.*
*Clear 5 · Unique 4 (both/and locks the swap test; loses 1 to HubSpot's partial flex claim) · True 5 (custom objects + afternoon-setup testimonials ship today).*

### 03 · BENEFITS

**Benefit 2 (HERO) — "Flexible like a doc. Real like a CRM. Finally, you don't have to choose."**
EDC 4/5/4 · *Formula: Before & After.* The squeeze stated as a benefit — Notion can't say "real like a CRM," Salesforce can't say "flexible like a doc."

**Benefit 1 — "Your CRM finally matches your business, not the other way around."**
EDC 5/5/4 · *Formula: Big Picture.* Custom objects zoomed out to: the tool fits you, not you fitting the tool.

**Benefit 3 — "Set it up your way, keep your data clean, and the AI can actually do the work."**
EDC 4/4/4 · *Formula: Benefit Progression.* AI-native becomes the **payoff** of the flexible data model, not a parallel claim. The AI works *because* the data is yours.

*Persona order for the builder ICP: 2 → 1 → 3.*
*Usage: Homepage sections, pillar slides, ad body copy, one benefit per outbound email.*
