# Attio: company profile

> Source: attio.com + public sources, June 2026. Verify before quoting on stage.

## One-liner (as they state it)

> "Attio is an AI-native CRM platform built for startups and builders that lets startup teams unify customer data, automate workflows and build custom solutions to improve their operations."

## What it is

A CRM platform. The pitch is "AI-native," meaning AI is built into the data model, context layer, and workflow engine from the start, rather than bolted onto a legacy CRM. The claim: AI does work (researches, surfaces signals, runs workflows), not just assists with data entry.

## Product capabilities (as listed)

- AI features: generative AI in-app, research agents, GTM acceleration
- Automations and workflows (AI-powered)
- Customizable data model: custom objects, enrichment, integrations, syncing
- Reporting: bar, line, pie, funnel charts
- Email and calendar sync, email sequences, advanced filters, data enrichment

## Who they say it's for

- Startups and builders
- Startup teams and high-growth tech companies
- Venture capital firms

## Traction and proof points

- 80,000+ startups use Attio (verify current number)
- Named customers: Lovable, Coca Cola, Flatfile, Modal, Union Square Ventures, Replicate, Railway, Public.com, Plain
- Testimonials: Modal ("the start of the next generation of CRM"), Union Square Ventures ("Attio feels like magic")

## Pricing (verify live)

- Free
- Plus: ~$29-36 per seat per month
- Pro: ~$69-86 per seat per month
- Enterprise: custom

## Stated differentiation

1. AI-native, not legacy retrofitted with AI
2. "The most customizable and flexible data model in CRM"
3. Purpose-built for modern startup speed and go-to-market motions
